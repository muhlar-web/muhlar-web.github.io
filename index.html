About me

Hi, I’m Miro! I’m a detail-oriented problem solver with a growing passion for data science and data engineering. My background in Intelligent Systems introduced me to machine learning and artificial intelligence—back before they became the buzzwords they are today. That early exposure sparked my fascination with using data to uncover insights and create impactful solutions, and I’m excited to continue developing my skills in this field.

One of the highlights of my journey so far was the opportunity to experience life and work in the USA while I was at university. It was an incredible chance to step outside my comfort zone, connect with people from diverse backgrounds, and grow both personally and professionally.

I’ve also had the chance to gain hands-on experience in companies listed in my resume, where I worked on practical solutions that sharpened my skills and fueled my enthusiasm for problem-solving. I’m eager to bring this passion, my curiosity to learn, and my fresh perspective to a team where I can contribute meaningfully and grow alongside experienced professionals.

What truly motivates me is the sense of accomplishment that comes from seeing the results of my work come to life. Whether it’s solving a tricky problem or discovering a fresh way to approach a challenge, I thrive on the process of exploration and creativity that leads to meaningful outcomes. I’m always eager to learn from those with more experience, contribute to impactful projects, and continue growing along the way. I’m also building my experience by working on personal and collaborative projects, which you can find in my GitHub section.

People often describe me as a good listener with a knack for bringing fresh perspectives to the table—qualities I value and aim to further develop throughout my career.

When I’m not immersed in work or picking up new skills, you’ll likely find me running, playing guitar (which I’ve taught myself!), diving into books of all kinds (as you can see below), hunting for vinyl records to expand my collection, or working on DIY projects. Sometimes, I even enjoy sealing my thoughts to paper, which helps me stay reflective and inspired. These hobbies fuel my curiosity and keep me energized in both life and work.

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or drop me an email—I’d love to chat about opportunities, ideas, or even swap book recommendations!
